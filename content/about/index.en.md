I am Vincent, a Cybersecurity student at Huazhong University of Science and Technology. I am currently learning my way around artificial intelligence and static program analysis, very much still a beginner in the field.

Most of what I do right now is simple: learn technical things, write notes, make unfinished research ideas a little clearer, and keep enough room for hobbies so life does not become only a list of tasks.

## This Blog

This place is meant to stay like a small digital salon: technical notes, research fragments, music, experiments, and the occasional private weather.

I want it to be quiet. Not a newsroom, not a tribunal, not a machine for chasing whatever is loud today. More like a room where useful things can be found again, and stray thoughts can have a place of their own.

The writing here will lean toward learning traces, technical records, reading, hobbies, ordinary days, and things that are not fully understood yet but still worth keeping.

## Current Direction

- Cybersecurity
- Artificial intelligence and static program analysis
- Program analysis tools, vulnerability understanding, code semantics, and whether AI can help people read code more reliably
